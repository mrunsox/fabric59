# ASC Shadow Debrief Script

Use this script for every internal and external pilot debrief during the ASC shadow rollout (`docs/asc-shadow-rollout.md`).

## How to run a debrief

Keep it short — 15–20 minutes. Open with one sentence of context ("we're running a quiet pilot on the assisted wizard and want your honest read"), then walk through the five questions below. Take verbatim notes; don't paraphrase into product language during the call. After the call, group answers across users and look for clusters. A single complaint is signal; three users hitting the same wall is a Phase 7 candidate.

## Questions

1. At which step did you feel the most lost or unsure?

2. Did any AI suggestion feel obviously wrong or unsafe?

3. Did you trust the handoff into the normal builder, or did it feel like starting over?

4. If we removed ASC tomorrow, what would you miss most? What would you be glad to lose?

5. Was there anything you expected ASC to do that it didn't, or anything it did that surprised you?
